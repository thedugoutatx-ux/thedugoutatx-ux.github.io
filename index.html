Card Grading Guide
10
Gem Mint — Perfect in every way. Sharp corners, flawless surface. Highest value.
9
Mint — Near perfect. Extremely popular grade with excellent value.
8
Near Mint-Mint — Very clean with minor imperfections. Great value grade.
Raw
Ungraded — Not submitted to a grading company. Most affordable option.
PSA, BGS (Beckett) and SGC are the three major grading companies. PSA commands the highest resale values.
